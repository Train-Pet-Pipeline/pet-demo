# Pain Point — missed signal, evening solitude

Photorealistic, cinematic 21:9 wide frame, early evening living room. A single tabby cat sits alone on the far end of a large cream-toned couch, body turned slightly away, tail curled around paws. The room is lit by a single muted clay-orange table lamp (#C65D3E), casting long warm shadows. The composition is sparse — cat small within the wide frame — conveying a sense of quiet solitude and a signal gone unnoticed. No owners present. Bone (#EDE8E1) and cream (#FFFCF7) walls, ink-dark (#1F1A17) floor.

Negative: sad or distressed animal, cold blue tones, multiple animals, human figures, text, logos, neon lighting.
